What We Need

Documentation required to begin the funding process

1) Quick Fit

  • Funding amount ($5M+ for tokenization)
  • Deal type: asset-backed, business-backed, or project
  • Jurisdiction (country/state)
  • For projects: ARV + current stage
  • For assets/business: verified value, revenue/cash flow

2) Capital Stack & Terms

  • Equity/cash invested to date
  • Participation offered (equity/profit share/revenue share)
  • Existing debt (lenders, balances, seniority)
  • Liability structure: recourse / limited / non-recourse
  • Ability to cover early carry costs

3) Project Package

  • Sources & uses: total budget, draw schedule
  • Permits/approvals status
  • Key counterparties (developer, GC, architect)
  • Pre-sales/pre-leases + evidence of demand
  • Exit plan (sale or refinance) + timeline

4) Initial Documents

  • Valuation/appraisal OR financial statements
  • Projects: ARV package + pitch deck/pro forma
  • Proof of ownership/title documentation
  • Borrower/entity details for compliance review
  • KYC/AML required prior to onboarding

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions. Timelines depend on file completeness, eligibility, and underwriting.

What is the minimum funding amount for tokenized funding?

The minimum facility size for tokenized funding is typically $5M+. For smaller amounts, we offer consulting-based introductions across 65+ funding programs.

How long does it take to close a tokenized funding deal?

Target close window is typically 35–50 days from a complete file submission (varies by deal complexity, jurisdiction, documentation readiness, and underwriting).

How quickly can banking instruments be issued?

For eligible transactions, the target turnaround can be ~7 banking days once compliance and file review are complete and approved, subject to issuing/confirming partner requirements.

Which instruments do you support?

We support LC, SBLC, BG, DLC, and BLC structures, including issuance, advising, and confirmation where applicable.

Do you guarantee funding or issuance?

No. Final decisions are made by independent third-party lenders and capital providers based on their own criteria. We do not guarantee approvals, pricing, timelines, or outcomes.
