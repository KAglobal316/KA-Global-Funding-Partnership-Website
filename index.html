Tokenization for Funding — Complete Process

Tokenization transforms your assets, business, or project into digital securities that can be efficiently placed with our network of 2,400+ institutional and private funding partners. This process ensures compliance, transparency, and faster capital deployment compared to traditional financing.

How Our Tokenization Funding Works

1. Asset-Based Lending (ABL)

We tokenize your real estate, land, or high-value assets, creating digital securities placed with institutional lenders. The asset serves as collateral, enabling up to 50%+ leverage.

2. Business-Based Funding (BBF)

Your business's contracts, receivables, and revenue streams are tokenized and placed with growth-focused institutional investors. Ideal for companies with strong performance metrics.

3. Project-Based Funding (PBF)

Development projects, infrastructure builds, and operations are tokenized based on projected cash flows and viability. Perfect for real estate development, energy projects, and infrastructure.

What We Need From You

1) Asset / Business / Project Profile

  • Funding request amount and specific use of capital
  • Asset type with current valuation support (appraisal, financials, or ARV)
  • Clear title / ownership documentation
  • Any supporting contracts, revenue history, or project documentation

2) Capital Stack & Terms

  • Equity/cash invested to date + additional equity available (if needed)
  • Participation offered (equity/profit share/revenue share/other)
  • Existing debt (lenders, balances, seniority, repayment status)
  • Liability structure: Recourse / limited recourse / non-recourse
  • Ability to cover early carry costs / reserves (as applicable)

3) Project Package (if applicable)

  • Sources & uses: total budget, draw schedule, milestone timeline
  • Permits/approvals status and key counterparties (developer, GC, architect/engineer)
  • Pre-sales/pre-leases (if any) + evidence of demand
  • Exit plan (sale or refinance) + target timeline

4) Initial Documents to Start Review

  • Valuation/appraisal OR financial statements
  • Projects: ARV package + pitch deck/pro forma
  • Proof of ownership/title documentation
  • Borrower/entity details for compliance review (KYC/AML required prior to onboarding)
